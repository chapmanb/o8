Variant scoring for the Archon Genomics XPrize

Follow this how-to video and submit your variant calls for scoring in 3 simple steps:

  1. Upload your variant VCF file, and optionally a BED file of regions to score, to GenomeSpace or Galaxy.
  2. Login below with your GenomeSpace username and password or Galaxy server information and API key.
  3. Select uploaded files, click score, and wait for your variant comparisons and statistics.
