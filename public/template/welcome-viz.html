o8: genetic changes we can believe in

o8 is a web-based platform to interactively explore and analyze human variation data. It easily interoperates with analysis frameworks like Galaxy and GenomeSpace to supplement existing tools with a reactive, intuitive way to identify biologically relevant changes.

It is an open source, freely available platform, extending some great biological software:

  • GATK: The Genome Analysis Toolkit from the Broad Institute
  • gemini: a framework for mining genome variation, from the Quinlan lab
  • Variant Effect Predictor: predicts functional consequences of variants, from the Ensembl team
  • bcbio.variation: a toolkit to compare and consolidate variation calls from multiple sources, developed at Harvard School of Public Health
